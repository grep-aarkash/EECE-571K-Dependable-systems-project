Types of Faults
Data Faults
1. Image noise
	a. Gaussian - mean(convolution filtering), median filtering, Gaussian smoothing
	b. Salt and pepper - median filter or a morphological filter
	c. Speckle noise
	d. Poisson Noise
2. Channel Occlusion
	a. Red
	b. Green
	c. Blue
3. Partial occlusion

Hardware Faults
1. Bit flip errors
2. Stuck-at faults
